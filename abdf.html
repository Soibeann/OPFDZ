Operation Dengue Free Zone



Operation 1

Survey and Mapping

We are going to conduct a thorough survey of the area to identify potential mosquito breeding sites. Mapping out these locations for easier monitoring and follow-up.




Operation 2

Community Awareness Campaign

Our Team is going to organize information drives to educate the community about the dangers of dengue and how to prevent it.
  • Distribute Flyers
  • Hold Meetings
  • Use Social Media




Operation 3

Source Reduction

Our Team is going to eliminate standing water where mosquitoes breed. This includes
  • Emtying
  • Covering
  • Treating Containers
  • Bukets
  • Tires
  • Flower Pots




Operation 4

Larvicide Application

Our Team is going to apply enviromentally-safe larvicides to water bodies that connot be drained, like
  • Ponds
  • Ditches
to kill mosquito larvae before they can mature.




Operation 5

Fogging Operations

Conducting fogging in the early morning or late afternoon to kill adult mosquitoes, particulary in areas with high mosquito populations or recent deque cases.




Operation 6

Monitoring and Evaluation

We are going to regularly check the new breeding sites and monitor mosquito populations. And keeping track of denque cases in the area to assess the effectiveness of the operation.
